The information published on Schoolspedia.in is intended to help parents, students, researchers, and other users find basic details about schools across India. While we make every reasonable effort to ensure accuracy, there are important limitations you should be aware of before relying on this data for any critical purpose.

1. Data Source and Accuracy

All school-level data on this website is sourced from the UDISE (Unified District Information System for Education) database maintained by the Ministry of Education, Government of India. District-level demographic data is sourced from the Census of India. These are official, government-maintained databases and represent the most authoritative sources of this information available in the public domain.

However, Schoolspedia.in is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially connected to the Ministry of Education, the Census of India, or any other government body. We are an independent informational website that presents publicly available government data in a more accessible format.

2. Data May Be Outdated

School data changes over time. Schools open, close, merge, upgrade their classes, or change their management type. The UDISE database is updated periodically, but there may be a lag between real-world changes and what is reflected in official records — and by extension, on this website. We update our data as new UDISE releases become available, but we cannot guarantee that every page reflects the current real-world status of every school.

Before making any important decision — such as enrolling your child in a school, or citing data in a formal document — please verify the information directly with the school concerned, the District Education Office, or the official UDISE portal at udiseplus.gov.in.
