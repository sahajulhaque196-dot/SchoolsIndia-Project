Reporting a Data Error

If you have found incorrect information about a school on this website, please email me with the following details:

  • The school name and UDISE code (shown on every school page)
  • The specific information you believe is incorrect
  • The correct information, along with any official source you can share

I take data accuracy seriously and will investigate and update the page as quickly as I can.
