Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025  ·  Last Updated: January 2025

At Schoolspedia.in, we respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about how this website operates. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have. Please read it carefully.

1. Information We Collect

Schoolspedia.in is primarily an informational directory. We do not require you to create an account, provide your name, or submit any personal information to access any content on this website.

However, like most websites, some information is collected automatically when you visit:

a) Usage Data (via Google Analytics)

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. This tool collects anonymised data such as your approximate location (country/city level), the pages you visit, how long you spend on each page, the type of device and browser you use, and how you arrived at our site (e.g., search engine, direct link). This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify you personally.

b) Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. Schoolspedia.in uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Analytics cookies — placed by Google Analytics to track usage patterns as described above.
  • Advertising cookies — placed by Google AdSense to serve relevant advertisements. These cookies may use information about your browsing history across websites to show you more relevant ads.

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some features on this and other websites.

2. Google AdSense and Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on this website. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our site and other sites on the internet.

You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings. You can also opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies by visiting Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page.

3. How We Use Information

The anonymised data we collect is used solely to:

  • Understand which pages and features are most useful to visitors
  • Improve the content and structure of the website
  • Diagnose technical issues
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to any third party. The only data shared is the anonymised analytics and advertising data described above, which is shared with Google under their own privacy policies.

5. External Links

Schoolspedia.in contains links to external websites including official government portals. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites and encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

6. Children's Privacy

Our website is an informational resource and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through this site, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
